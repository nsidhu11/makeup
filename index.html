About Us

Rare Glam Studio was established in 2010 with the mission to redefine beauty and elegance. Founded by a passionate group of artists, our studio aims to bring out the natural charm in every client, enhancing their features with personalized makeup experiences. Our journey began with a simple idea: that makeup is more than just a routine‚Äîit is an art form, a confidence booster, and a way to embrace your unique beauty. At Rare Glam Studio, we pride ourselves on our attention to detail, using only the highest quality products and techniques that are as unique as our clients. Our talented team consists of professionally trained makeup artists and stylists who are committed to delivering an unmatched level of service. Each makeup session is tailored to your specific needs, ensuring that you receive a look that best complements your individuality, whether it's for a special occasion, a photoshoot, or just a day to treat yourself. From glamorous bridal makeovers that make every bride shine on her big day to chic everyday looks for the modern woman, Rare Glam Studio offers an unforgettable beauty journey. We love to collaborate with our clients to make sure they feel heard, comfortable, and pampered every step of the way. Over the years, we have worked with countless clients, each with unique stories, personalities, and styles, which drives our passion for what we do. Our dedicated team believes in creating a warm, luxurious environment where each individual feels valued, confident, and truly glamorous. With an emphasis on creativity, professionalism, and precision, Rare Glam Studio is your go-to destination for all things beauty. Our goal is simple: to make you feel beautiful inside and out, and to make every visit to our studio an experience worth cherishing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makeup brands do you use?

We use a range of high-quality brands such as MAC, NARS, and Charlotte Tilbury to ensure the best results for our clients.

How far in advance should I book a bridal makeup session?

We recommend booking at least three months in advance to ensure availability on your special day.

Do you offer makeup trials?

Yes, we offer makeup trials for brides to help them achieve their perfect look before the big day.

Can I bring my own products?

Absolutely, we encourage our clients to bring their favorite products if they wish to use them.

Do you travel for makeup sessions?

Yes, we provide on-location services for bridal and other special occasions. Travel fees may apply.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require a 24-hour notice for cancellations to avoid any charges.
